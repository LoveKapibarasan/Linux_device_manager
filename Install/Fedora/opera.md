### Speed Dial
* The Speed Dial is a visual set of entries made from your most visited sites or installed extensions. Speed Dial entries appear as thumbnails that link to the page. You can organize your favorite sites into folders.



### Setting
`Alt` + `P`

* Default Search Engine
* Hide speed dial
* Disable ads [URL](https://forums.opera.com/topic/66391/disable-start-page-banner-ads/10)
